This is an AppImage of the Linux Creative Suite AppPack installer. It offers alternatives to the Adobe Creative Cloud suite of software to be selected and installed with a GUI and provides custom icon and custom program grouping in the Linux DE application menu.

The following applications are available to be installed by the distribution's native package manager or flatpack as a fallback.

*   GIMP - Alternative to Photoshop
    
*   Inkscape - Alternative to Illustrator
    
*   Kdenlive - Alternative to Premiere Pro
    
*   Blender - Alternative to After Effects + Dimension
    
*   Krita - Alternative to Photoshop (Digital Art)
    
*   LibreOffice Draw - Alternative to Illustrator (Technical)
    
*   Scribus - Alternative to InDesign
    
*   OpenToonz - Alternative to Animate
    
*   Synfig Studio - Alternative to After Effects (2D)
    
*   Audacity - Alternative to Audition
    
*   darktable - Alternative to Lightroom
    
*   RawTherapee - Alternative to Lightroom
    
*   BlueGriffon - Alternative to Dreamweaver
    
*   Figma - Alternative to XD
    
*   Penpot - Alternative to XD
    
*   Okular - Alternative to Acrobat Reader DC
